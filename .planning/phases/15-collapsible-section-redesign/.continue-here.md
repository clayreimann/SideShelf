<decisions_made>

- Used `collapsible-sizer` (separate absolutely-positioned measurement view) instead of
  trying to measure inside the clip container. This is the only reliable approach across
  Paper and Fabric renderers — both height and maxHeight propagate as Yoga constraints.
- Children render twice (sizer + visible container). Acceptable trade-off for correctness.
  Sizer is invisible and non-interactive; overhead is minimal.
- Used `maxHeight` on the visible Animated.View (not `height`) to avoid Yoga constraint
  propagation to children as a secondary defense.
- Gradient uses hue-matched transparent (`bgTransparent`) to avoid gray midpoint fringing
  in premultiplied alpha interpolation.
- `isExpanded` is ONLY set by `toggle()` — never in `handleSizerLayout`. This prevents
  RenderHtml's async multi-pass renders from prematurely setting expanded state.

</decisions_made>
