**1. [Rule 1 - Bug] Flat payload structure in traceDump**

- **Found during:** Implementation (traceDump.ts)
- **Issue:** Plan template called `trace.exportTrace({ dumpReason, appVersion, platform })` and wrote the `TraceExport` object directly. The `TraceExport` type nests meta under a `meta` field, so `parsed.dumpReason` would be `undefined` — failing 3 of 4 traceDump tests.
- **Fix:** Implemented a custom flat payload spreading meta keys (appVersion, platform, dumpReason, rejectionEvent) to the root alongside exportedAt and records.
