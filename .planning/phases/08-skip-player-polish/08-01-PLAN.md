<objective>
Replace the AsyncStorage-on-mount interval state in FullScreenPlayer with Zustand `useSettings()` so that the selected skip interval persists across app restarts.

Purpose: The skip forward and backward intervals are already persisted in AsyncStorage by `settingsSlice` on startup — FullScreenPlayer bypasses this by reading AsyncStorage directly into local state. Switching to `useSettings()` makes the Zustand store the single source of truth and eliminates the redundant read. Wiring `handleJumpForward`/`handleJumpBackward` to call `updateJumpForwardInterval`/`updateJumpBackwardInterval` persists the user's selection immediately.

Output: FullScreenPlayer uses `useSettings()` for interval state; long-press menu selection updates the stored default interval.
</objective>
